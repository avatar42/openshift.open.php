wp Whois Proxy: Access Denied

Your host has exceeded $n (maximum number of) visits per $p (configurable period) for this WP Whois Proxy service. We have placed this limit on access to help prevent its abuse by spammers and other lowlife types who like to use the various WHOIS databases to harvest information.

If you have a true need for additional access, here's the deal: this is free software, so download your copy and run it on your own system.

If you really need to be excepted from this limitation, you can send me an email including the following items:

  • Contact information (name, email and phone number).
  • The IP address for which you are requesting open access.
  • The reason you are requesting more access (and why you can't run your own copy.

If you don't include the above information, you will be ignored.

We apologize for the strict guidelines but due to the increasing amount of junk floating around on the 'net we want to do our part to help keep it clean.
